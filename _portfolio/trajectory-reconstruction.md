**Bayesian Deep Learning** · Spring 2025

Reconstructing high-frequency robot trajectories from sparse keyframes with calibrated uncertainty. Compared per-joint Gaussian Process baselines against a masked variational autoencoder on a real SO-101 manipulator dataset — the VAE wins at sparse keyframe rates (≤ 1 Hz) by exploiting dataset-wide motion structure.
